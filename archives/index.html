罗阳 (Luo,Yang)，河海大学学士，厦门大学博士。博士毕业后曾在日本大阪大学 (Osaka University), 美国肯塔基大学 (University of Kentucky) 做博士后。现为云南大学物理与天文学院天文学系副教授 (Associate Professor)。Yang did his Ph.D in Xiamen University. He worded as a postdoc in Osaka University, and University of Kentucky. He is now an associate professor in the Department of Astronomy in Yunnan University.

Research Interests

  • Formation of Supermassive Black Hole Seed at High Redshift: Numerical Simulations, Intermediate-Mass BH Formation, First Quasar, BH Growth, Gravitational Wave, TDE
  • Black Hole Binaries, AGN Fueling
  • X-ray Astronomy: X-ray Hot Gas, Circum-Galactic Medium, Galactic Feedback
  • High Energy Phenomenon: Accretion Disks, X-ray Binaries, Gamma-ray Bursts

Skills & Proficiency

  • 3D Hydrodynamic Numerical Simulations. Familiar with code Enzo, Gadget, Gizmo
  • Radiative Transfer modeling, ray-tracing, FLD
  • X-ray data reduction and analysis. Familiar with Chandra, XMM-Newton grating and imaging spectroscopy. Skilled with Xspec and absorption line analysis.
  • Computer skills. Xstar, Cloudy, C/C++, Python, YT.
