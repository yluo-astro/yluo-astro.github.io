罗阳 (Luo,Yang)，厦门大学博士。博士毕业后曾在日本大阪大学 (Osaka University), 美国肯塔基大学 (University of Kentucky) 做博士后。现为云南大学物理与天文学院天文学系副教授 (Associate Professor)。Yang obtained his Ph.D degree from Xiamen University. Then he worked as a Postdoc at Osaka University, and University of Kentucky. He is now an Associate Professor in the Department of Astronomy at Yunnan University.

Research Interests

  • From Black Hole Seed Formation to the First Quasar: Numerical Simulations of BH Seed Formation, Direct Collapse to BH Seeds, Intermediate-Mass BH Formation, BH Growth, Supermassive Star Formation, Self-gravitational Disk Formation
  • Supper Massive Black Hole Binaries: Circum-Binary Accretion Disk, AGN Fueling, BH Mergers
  • High Redshift Observations: Observations of First Quasar, Circum-Galactic Medium, Galactic Feedback, AGN in Dwarf Galaxies
  • Theoretical Modeling: Accretion Disks, Star Formation in AGN Disks, Metal Enrichment in the First Quasar

Skills & Proficiency

  • 3D Hydrodynamic Numerical Simulations. Familiar with code Enzo, Gadget/Gizmo, Athena++, and Arepo
  • Radiative Transfer Modeling: ray-tracing, FLD
  • MHD modeling
  • X-ray Data Reduction and Analysis. Familiar with Chandra, XMM-Newton grating and imaging spectroscopy. Skilled with Xspec/Astropy, Cloudy modeling, Lya radiative transfer modeling ...
